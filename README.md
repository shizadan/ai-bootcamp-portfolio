## Projects

### 1. Diabetes Dataset EDA — Week 1 Assignment
**File:** `wk1_project.ipynb`  
**Dataset:** Pima Indians Diabetes Dataset (Kaggle)  
**Tools:** Python, Pandas, Matplotlib, Seaborn  

**What I did:**
- Loaded and cleaned a real medical dataset (768 patients, 9 features)
- Detected and fixed biologically invalid zero values
- Answered 5 analytical questions using Pandas
- Built 10 charts across Matplotlib and Seaborn
- Identified glucose level as the strongest predictor of diabetes

**Key Insight:** Diabetic patients averaged 141 mg/dL glucose compared 
to 110 mg/dL for non-diabetic patients — a 31 mg/dL difference.

### 2. COVID-19 Global Analysis — Independent Study
**File:** `covid19_analysis.ipynb`  
**Dataset:** COVID-19 Data (Kaggle)  
**Tools:** Python, Pandas, Matplotlib, Seaborn  

**What I did:**
- Analysed 306,429 records spanning January 2020 to May 2021
- Tracked global case growth trends over time
- Compared top 10 most affected countries
- Calculated and compared death rates across countries
- Told the complete COVID-19 story for Africa

**Key Insight:** Countries with the most confirmed cases were NOT 
the deadliest. Limited testing infrastructure in smaller nations 
created artificially high death rates — revealing how data quality 
shapes what numbers actually show.
